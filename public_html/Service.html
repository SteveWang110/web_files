Service

I am currently a Teaching Assistant at Texas A&M University. While this is a job, and I get paid for it, I absolutely love helping undergraduate students struggling in engineering courses. I try my best to help those that need help, and am elated when my advice benefits them.

I have gone to church ever since I was a little kid, and often there were volunteer opportunities available. For example, one of the most notable volunteering I have done was helping people rebuild houses that were damaged by Hurricane Harvey back in 2017. Some other volunteering I did include helping out at the Houston Food Bank, where we prepared lunches for kids, as well as multiple different fundraisers for children.

In the summer of 2021, I worked for a company called RiceTec, and worked a full-time summer job for 3 months, with 40 hour weeks. This job helped me learn how to manage my time efficiently, because an 8-5 job is a huge time commitment, so I had to learn how to use my time wisely. My role in this company was an assistant scientist, as I collected samples of plant DNA, and I also managed the greenhouse, keeping the plants alive and cross breeding them.
